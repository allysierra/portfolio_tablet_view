I went to Mexico for Christmas of 2020 to visit my partner's family.
We were there for almost three weeks!
Most of the time was spent with family, as we were extremely cautious
of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Here are my tips if you're planning to travel to Mexico:
Don't drink the tap water,
don't flush toilet paper (pipes are too narrow),
bring your sunscreen,
and study up on your Spanish!
