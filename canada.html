Canada

I went to Canada for my twenty-first birthday in November of 2019.
I only stayed for one night, but I enjoyed some great eats and drinks!

Here are my tips if you're planning to travel to Canada:
Clean out your car if you're driving across the border
(they will search your car if it's messy!),
prepare to see a lot of nineteen year-olds drinking,
and don't forget that you might lose cell service!
